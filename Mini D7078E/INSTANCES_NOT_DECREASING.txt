THE REASON
================================================================================

Scale-in has strict requirements:

1. CPU must drop BELOW 30% (not just low)
2. Must STAY below 30% for 5 CONSECUTIVE minutes
3. Only THEN will instances terminate

Why 5 minutes?
  ‚îî‚îÄ CloudWatch evaluates alarms every 60 seconds
  ‚îî‚îÄ Needs 5 consecutive evaluations (5 periods)
  ‚îî‚îÄ Safety feature: prevents thrashing (adding/removing too fast)
